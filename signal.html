// Transmission

Transmitting from
the underground.

Subspace is a Birmingham-based underground drum and bass label and events collective. Born from a shared obsession with dark, heavy frequencies, we exist to give a platform to the rawest sounds coming out of Birmingham and beyond.

From heavyweight label releases to intimate studio sessions and basement raves, Subspace operates at the intersection of music, community, and pure underground culture. No compromise. No polish. Just the frequencies as they fell.
